新宿駅

Shinjuku-eki, Tōkyō

A stamp reflecting on rather different beginnings to the current massive, sprawling station

Shinjuku station, East Exit

Many of the stamps depict different times, but for me this contrast is one of the most stark.

This stamp was captured on Sunday, September 25th 2016, at the East exit (not Central East) of Shinjuku. It was downstairs, inside a ticket office in the right corner as you go in.
